# ASO Guidelines

When drafting the store listing, you must adhere strictly to these platform rules.

## Global Rules
1.  **No Competitor Trademarks:** Never use a competitor's name in your title, subtitle, or keyword field. You can hint at them in the long description (e.g., "Switching from the green elephant app?").
2.  **No Price Mentions in Titles:** Do not use "Free" or "$0" in the Title or Subtitle.

## Apple App Store Limits
*   **Title:** 30 characters maximum. (Must include the brand name and top keyword).
*   **Subtitle:** 30 characters maximum. (Must be an action-oriented hook).
*   **Promotional Text:** 170 characters maximum. (Shows above the fold without expanding. Crucial for conversion).
*   **Description:** 4000 characters maximum. (HTML is NOT supported. Use standard text spacing and bullet points like `•`).
*   **Keywords Field:** 100 characters maximum. (Comma-separated, no spaces after commas to save room. E.g., `habit tracker,adhd planner,focus timer`).

## Google Play Limits
*   **App Name:** 30 characters maximum.
*   **Short Description:** 80 characters maximum. (The most critical text for Android conversion).
*   **Full Description:** 4000 characters maximum. (Basic HTML is supported, but prefer simple text formatting and emojis for cross-platform consistency).
