# Competitor Brief: Evernote

## The Top 3 Broken Promises
1.  **"Fast Note Taking"** -> Users complain the app takes 10 seconds to open, causing them to forget their thoughts before they can write them down.
2.  **"Sync Everywhere"** -> Users complain that sync often duplicates notes or fails silently while offline, resulting in lost data.
3.  **"All-in-one Workspace"** -> Users complain the interface has become too cluttered with tasks, calendars, and AI tools they never use, making it impossible to find their actual notes.
